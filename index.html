Yu Liu (刘羽)

Yu Liu is a Full Professor and the Head of the Department of Biomedical Engineering at Hefei University of Technology, Hefei, China. He is the director of Multi-modal Information Fusion and Perception (MIFP) Research Group. His current research interests include image fusion, medical image processing, computer vision, etc. He has published over 140 scientific articles in prestigious journals and conferences including IEEE TPAMI, IJCV, IEEE TIP, MedIA, INFFUS, CVPR, ICCV, etc. His publications have totally received over 20000 citations (Google Scholar). He is serving as an Associate Editor or Editorial Board Member for journals including IEEE TIP, INFFUS, IEEE SPL, etc. He was a recipient of the IEEE Instrumentation and Measurement Society Andi Chi Best Paper Award in 2020 and the IET Image Processing Premium (Best Paper) Award in 2017. He was identified as a Clarivate Highly Cited Researcher (since 2023) and an Elsevier Highly Cited Chinese Researcher (since 2020).
